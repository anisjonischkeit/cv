  • I uplifted the Origin Design System (Component Library) to the latest major dependencies (MUI, React, Typescript, Babel, etc) and provided a migration path that allows most microsites to upgrade with very little effort. The library is used by over 100 apps, contains around 150 reusable UI components that were 2 - 5 years outdated, and was updated with almost 0 visual regression. The project has brought new life, excitement and momentum to our Design System, and a wave of optimism around our ability to iterate on it.
